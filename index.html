Getting started

Three uploads. Instant insight.

No account. No app to install. Open in any browser.

1

Export your CGM data

Go to LibreView or Dexcom Clarity and download your CSV export. Takes about 30 seconds.

FreeStyle Libre 3 · Dexcom G6/G7 · Stelo
2

Upload your period data

Export from Apple Health, Clue, or any app that gives you a date list of period starts and ends.

Your data never leaves your browser

Glycles runs entirely client-side. When you upload your CGM CSV or period file, it is parsed and processed locally in your browser. Nothing is transmitted to any server. There is no account, no cloud storage, and no tracking. Close the tab and the data is gone. This is a deliberate design choice, not a limitation.
